2. Vibe Coding - UI Structure & Layout

Learn the structure and layout principles of user interface web pages. You'll build 10 different types of professional projects that showcase your skills.

Landing Page for Small Business or Course
A single page website that introduces your business or course to visitors. Think of it as a digital business card that tells people what you offer and encourages them to take action - like signing up or contacting you.
Portfolio & Personal Branding Page
Your online resume and showcase. This is where you display your best work, skills, and achievements so potential clients or employers can see what you're capable of. It's like a digital portfolio that represents your professional identity.
Event, Workshop, or Bootcamp Page
A dedicated page for promoting your event, workshop, or training program. It provides all the important details - date, location, what attendees will learn, and a way for people to register. Like a digital invitation and registration form combined.
Product or Service Showcase
A beautiful page that highlights what you're selling or the services you offer. It shows the benefits, features, and why people should choose you. Similar to a product catalog or service menu, but interactive and visually appealing.
Contact or Lead Generation Page
A page designed to collect information from visitors who are interested in your business. People fill out a form with their contact details, and you get potential customers. It's like having a digital receptionist that collects visitor information 24/7.
Visual UI Template or UI Kit
A collection of pre-designed website elements (buttons, forms, cards) that you can reuse in different projects. Think of it as a toolbox of ready-made design pieces that save you time and ensure consistency across your websites.
Blog or News Page (Static)
A page where you can share articles, news, or updates with your audience. It's organized like a magazine or newspaper, with a list of articles and individual pages for each post. Perfect for sharing knowledge, company news, or industry insights.
Simple Web Campaign / "Link in Bio" Page
A simple page with multiple links, perfect for social media profiles or marketing campaigns. Instead of having just one link in your Instagram bio, you create one page that leads to multiple destinations - your website, products, social accounts, etc. Like a digital directory.
Simple Dashboard (Mockup)
A control panel that displays important information at a glance - like statistics, charts, and key numbers. Similar to a car's dashboard showing speed and fuel, but for business data. Helps you or your clients see what's happening quickly.
Multi-Section Information Page
A comprehensive page that organizes lots of information into clear sections. Like a brochure or guidebook, but online. Perfect for explaining complex topics, company information, or detailed services. Visitors can easily navigate between different sections to find what they need.

Each project you build will have its code published on GitHub and a live demo link. At the end of the program, you'll have a professional portfolio containing all 9 projects with their GitHub code links and live demo links.
